Banana_Bread

Description

This is one of my personal favorites discovered by Emily during welcome week 2023. It was truly a master piece eating after helping out during the Radio City event

All credits to Trader Joes for having one of the best banana bread

Ingridients

  • Lots of love
  • Bananas
  • All purpose flour
  • mixing bowl

Steps

  1. First fill all other ingridients with love
  2. Put in the mashed bananas and flour in the mixing bowl
  3. mix and cook in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes
